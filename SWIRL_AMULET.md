# The Swirl Amulet - story + add-on copy

## The Story - The Swirl and the Still Point

The Whirlpool is the card that scares people most. A spiral with no bottom, drawn in dark water, pulling everything toward a center you cannot see.

Calypso turns it face-up between you. THE WHIRLPOOL. She does not flinch.

"Everyone fights the spin," she says. "That is why they drown in it. The whirlpool is not trying to kill you. It is trying to teach you where the center is."

She touches the painted spiral at its heart - the one still point, the eye, where the water turns but does not move.

"Here," she says. "Everything spins around this. The card, the sea, your life. The still point is always at the center. You cannot stop the turning. You can only learn to find the place inside it that does not turn."

From around her neck she lifts a pendant. Blown glass, blue as deep water, and inside it - the swirl. The same spiral, caught whole, with its still point at the middle.

She sets it in your palm. It is heavier than it looks, and cooler.

"When you cannot find the center," she says, "hold this. The whirlpool is not outside you. It never was. The spin is the world. The still point is you."

She lets you keep it.

Then she takes a second one from the dark - identical, twin, the same blue swirl sealed inside.

"Some spins are shared," she says. "This one is for the person who is your still point. The one who, when everything turns, does not turn away. You keep one. You give one. Two centers. One spin."

She closes your fingers around both.

"Do not lose the center," she says. "And do not lose the one who holds the other half."
