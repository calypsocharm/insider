# The Swirl Amulet - story + add-on copy

## The Story - The Swirl and the Oracle

The Whirlpool is the card people fear. A spiral with no bottom, dark water turning toward a center you cannot see.

Calypso turns it face-up between you. THE WHIRLPOOL. She does not flinch. She looks at it the way you would look at an old friend who tells hard truths.

"This is not a danger," she says. "This is the oracle. When I do not know, I come here. I speak into the spin, and the spin turns the question until an answer surfaces at the center."

She touches the painted spiral's heart - the still eye, where the water turns but does not move.

"Every answer I have ever given you came through here. The whirlpool does not drown. It listens. It takes your question, turns it, and gives back what you could not find on your own."

From around her neck she lifts a pendant. Blown glass, blue as deep water, and inside it - the swirl. The same spiral, sealed whole, its center glowing faintly.

"A piece of it," she says. "The oracle does not live only in the card. It lives in the water, and it lives in this. When you cannot come to the sea, hold this. Ask. Listen for the center. The answer will turn up."

She sets it in your palm. Heavier than it looks, cooler.

Then she lifts a second - identical, twin, the same blue swirl.

"This one is not for you to keep," she says. "It is for the one you would ask alongside. Two who share the oracle's ear hear more than one. Give it to them. Ask together."

She closes your fingers around both.

"The sea answers anyone who waits at the center," she says. "You simply had to be given the way in."
