# Calypso Star - Card Face Spec (all three card types)

Every card prints: **poker index** (rank+suit, top-left + bottom-right rotated) and the **point value**. The title block differs by type. Background differs Lenormand vs. minor.

## 1. Pip cards (Aces + 6-10 = the 36 Lenormand, minus courts)
- Poker index: corners (e.g., 10♠)
- Title: the Lenormand true name, e.g., **THE SHIP**
- Value: +1
- Center: the oceanic scene for that symbol
- Background: Lenormand ground (richer, vignette/plate feel)

## 2. Minor cards (2,3,4,5 of each suit = the 16 custom "missing verses")
- Poker index: corners (e.g., 5♠)
- Title: the custom true name, e.g., **THE STORM**
- Value: -2
- Center: the small domestic ocean symbol
- Background: minor ground (calmer, plainer, more open)

## 3. Court cards (J / Q / K = the gods) - connect ALL THREE layers
- Poker index: corners (e.g., J♥)
- Title line 1 (FIRST): the **Lenormand name** - e.g., **THE HEART** (because J of Hearts = Lenormand #24, Heart)
- Title line 2 (UNDER): the **god or goddess** - e.g., **THE SIREN**
- Value: +2
- Center: the deity portrait (enthroned in its realm)
- Background: Lenormand ground (courts are Lenormand too)

### Court example - Jack of Hearts
```
J♥ (corner)                              [badge/value +2]

        [ The Siren portrait - center ]

            THE HEART        <- Lenormand name first
            THE SIREN        <- god/goddess under
                                         J♥ (corner)
```
All three layers on one face: poker (J♥ index), Lenormand (THE HEART), god (THE SIREN), plus the value.

### All 12 courts (Lenormand name -> god), from the spec
- J♥  THE HEART      -> The Siren        (+2)
- Q♥  THE STORK      -> The Brine        (+1)
- K♥  THE HOUSE      -> The Leviathan    (+2)
- J♠  THE CHILD      -> The Drifter      (+1)
- Q♠  THE BOUQUET    -> The Tidewatcher  (+2)
- K♠  THE LILY       -> The Undertow     (+1)
- J♦  THE SCYTHE     -> The Lantern-Hermit (-2)
- Q♦  THE CROSSROADS -> The Reef Mother  (0)
- K♦  THE FISH       -> The Lighthouse   (+1)
- J♣  THE WHIP       -> The Rising       (-1)
- Q♣  THE SNAKE      -> The Oracle       (-2)
- K♣  THE CLOUDS     -> The Open Sea     (-1)

## Background rule (recap)
- Lenormand cards (the 36, including courts): richer "scene plate" ground.
- Minor cards (the 16): calmer, plainer ground.
- Both grounds stay neutral/abyss-toned so the suit=realm color still reads from the bottom band + suit glyph + art.
