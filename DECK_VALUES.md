## Final-score ladder
- **+4 or higher:** Yes
- **+1 to +3:** Probably yes
- **0:** Unclear / conditional / not yet decided
- **−1 to −3:** Probably no
- **−4 or lower:** No

## Spread size note
Use an **odd card count** (3 / 5 / 7) so the sum can't tie at 0. 5 is the sweet spot for a yes/no verdict.

## Optional reversal house-rules (guidebook only; not the official rule)
- **No reversals (official):** use the printed value exactly.
- **Flip:** +2 becomes −2.
- **Weaken:** +2 becomes +1; −2 becomes −1.
- **Blockage:** reversed cards score 0 but still inform the interpretation.
